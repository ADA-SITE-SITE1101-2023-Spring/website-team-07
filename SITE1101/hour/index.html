Hour of Code

Greetings to all. We are students of SITE at ADA! You will learn about our "Hours of Code" assignment, which required us to instruct kids and then share our insights via Code.org. We are group 07. Arif Mammadli, Zulfiyya Safarli, Jahangir Khalafov, and Alsari Mohamed Abbas Saad Eldin are the members of our team. The school No. 264 in Gunashli hosted an Hour of Code event that was arranged by our team. Despite our early Internet connection issues in the classroom, we were still able to host a successful event. We start by introducing ADA University, then go into great detail about our faculty, programming languages, and basic Code.org information. Then we began carrying out the activities with the kids. They finished all the tasks in about 30 minutes since they worked quickly and intelligently. Students who finished all the problems were awarded diplomas for their participation at the end of the event!
